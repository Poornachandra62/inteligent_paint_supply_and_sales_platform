## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Presentation

### **Before You Start:**
1. **Test the demo thoroughly** - ensure internet connection for AI features
2. **Have backup screenshots** in case of technical issues
3. **Practice the demo flow** - know exactly which buttons to click
4. **Prepare for internet issues** - have offline features ready to show

### **During Presentation:**
1. **Speak confidently** - you built something impressive
2. **Use business terminology** - "ROI", "scalability", "market opportunity"
3. **Emphasize real data** - this differentiates you from other projects
4. **Show enthusiasm** - your passion will be contagious

### **Body Language:**
1. **Stand straight and face the audience**
2. **Use hand gestures while explaining features**
3. **Make eye contact with different people**
4. **Smile and show confidence**

### **Technical Demo Tips:**
1. **Keep browser windows organized**
2. **Increase font size for better visibility**
3. **Explain what you're clicking before you click**
4. **Narrate what's happening on screen**

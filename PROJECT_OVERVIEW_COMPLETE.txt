1. PROJECT BRIEF
================================================================================

ChromaVerse is an advanced, AI-powered business management system designed 
specifically for the paint industry. It provides comprehensive tools for 
managing inventory, processing sales, analyzing customer behavior, and making 
data-driven decisions through intelligent insights.

The system serves three distinct user roles:
- Business Owners (Strategic Management)
- Salespersons (Point of Sale & Customer Service)
- Distributors (Supply Chain & Logistics)

Each role has customized dashboards, features, and AI assistance tailored to 
their specific responsibilities and workflows.
